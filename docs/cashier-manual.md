# Cashier User Manual - POS System

## Welcome to the POS System

This manual will guide you through using the Point of Sale (POS) system as a cashier. The system is designed to be intuitive and efficient for processing sales quickly and accurately.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Launch the POS application on your computer
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Login" or press Enter
4. The system will load your workspace

### Understanding the Interface

The POS interface consists of three main areas:

- **Left Panel**: Product catalog with search and categories
- **Right Panel**: Shopping cart and checkout
- **Top Bar**: Status indicators and settings

## Processing a Sale

### Step 1: Adding Products to Cart

#### Method 1: Search by Name
1. Click in the search box at the top of the product catalog
2. Type the product name or barcode
3. Select the product from the results
4. The product will be added to your cart

#### Method 2: Browse Categories
1. Click on a category from the dropdown menu
2. Browse the products in that category
3. Click "Add to Cart" on the desired product

#### Method 3: Barcode Scanning
1. Click the "📷 Scan Barcode" button
2. Allow camera access if prompted
3. Point the camera at the product barcode
4. The product will be automatically added to cart
5. Or manually enter the barcode in the input field

### Step 2: Managing the Cart

#### Adjusting Quantities
- Click the "+" button to increase quantity
- Click the "-" button to decrease quantity
- Type a specific number in the quantity field

#### Removing Items
- Click the "×" button next to any item to remove it

#### Applying Discounts
- Enter a discount percentage in the "Discount (%)" field during checkout
- The discount will be applied to the subtotal

### Step 3: Checkout Process

1. Review all items in your cart
2. Verify quantities and prices
3. Click "Checkout" button
4. Select payment method:
   - **Cash**: Enter amount received, system calculates change
   - **Card**: Process card payment
   - **Digital**: Select digital wallet option

### Step 4: Completing the Sale

1. Confirm payment details
2. Click "Complete Sale"
3. Receipt will be displayed
4. Choose to print receipt or email it
5. Click "New Sale" to start over

## Advanced Features

### Offline Operation

The POS system works even without internet connection:

- All sales are stored locally
- System syncs automatically when connection is restored
- No interruption to sales process
- Status indicator shows "Offline" in top bar

### Product Information

- **Stock Levels**: Check available quantity before selling
- **Product Details**: View descriptions and specifications
- **Pricing**: Current price is always displayed
- **Categories**: Organize products by type

### Customer Management

- Add customer information for receipts
- Track customer preferences
- Apply customer-specific discounts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Product Not Found
- Check spelling in search
- Try searching by barcode
- Verify product exists in catalog
- Contact manager if product should be added

#### Payment Processing Issues
- Verify payment method is selected
- Check internet connection for card payments
- Ensure card reader is connected
- Try alternative payment method

#### Printer Not Working
- Check paper supply
- Verify printer connection
- Restart printer if necessary
- Contact technical support

#### System Running Slow
- Close unnecessary applications
- Restart the POS application
- Check internet connection
- Contact IT support if persistent

### Glossary

- **SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit - unique product identifier
- **UPC**: Universal Product Code - barcode standard
- **POS**: Point of Sale - checkout system
- **SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit
- **EOF**: End of File
- **Sync**: Synchronization of local and server data
- **Batch**: Group of transactions processed together
- **Void**: Cancel a transaction
- **Refund**: Return money for returned items
- **Discount**: Price reduction applied to sale
